Product Details


Coastal Reverie, inspired by Duke Zeronis, is a fragrance that echoes the straightforward and refreshing nature of ocean breezes with a hint of intriguing depth. Opening with top notes of crisp oceanic freshness, the scent mirrors Zeronis's clear and direct thinking. As it evolves, a heart of sophisticated liquor rose and whispers of exotic spices adds a touch of intriguing allure. The base notes of smooth sandalwood and amber create a lingering and contemplative trail, embodying the essence of Duke Zeronis—a scent that mirrors the coastal charm and straightforward sophistication of a nobleman by the sea
